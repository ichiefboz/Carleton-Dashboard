Welcome!

This applet contains a live dashboard of facility hours at Carleton College.

Facility hours may vary from what is posted here, and are subject to change without notice - especially during finals and breaks. Always check with the facility in question to confirm their current hours.

Jump to: Sayles - Dining - Libe - Academic Support - ITS - Recreation - Business/Admin - Other

This information refreshes every 30 seconds.

Email boslert at carleton with feedback.

Notice something wrong?

If you notice something is wrong - such as a missing building, inaccurate hours, or misspelled office - please shoot me an email and I can take a look.

Please note that many facilities' hours will not display correctly during FINALS and BREAKS. This is particularly true for Sayles and the Libe. That being said, please do email me with updated information if something is wrong.

About this site

I wrote the 150 lines of code and ~100 lines of markup that make up this page in about a three-day span as a fun project. The site is named "Is the LDC open?" because my roommate and my friends kept asking me that question during New Student Week.

Side note, who eats dinner at 4:45?

I'm using MaterializeCSS for layout, and the icons and font are provided by Google. Other than that, everything was written by hand. (I'll publish the source soon for those of you who program in PHP.)
